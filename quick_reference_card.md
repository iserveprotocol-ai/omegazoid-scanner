## ðŸŽ¯ Entry Strategies

### 1. Pullback Entry (Best for Beginners)
- Wait for price to pull back to 20 or 50 EMA
- RSI resets to 40-50
- Enter when price bounces with volume
- Stop below the EMA

### 2. Breakout Entry
- Price breaks above resistance
- Volume 2x average
- Enter on breakout or retest
- Stop below breakout level

### 3. Moving Average Cross
- 20 EMA crosses above 50 EMA
- Price above both
- Enter on cross or first pullback
- Stop below 50 EMA

## ðŸ›¡ï¸ Risk Management Rules

**The 1-2% Rule:** Never risk more than 1-2% per trade

**Position Size Formula:**
```
Position Size = (Account Risk $) / (Entry - Stop Loss)
```

**Example:**
- Account: $10,000
- Risk: 2% = $200
- Entry: $50
- Stop: $48
- Risk per share: $2
- Position: $200 / $2 = 100 shares

**Stop Loss Placement:**
- Below support levels
- Below moving averages
- Below swing lows
- 1.5-2x ATR below entry

**Profit Targets:**
- Minimum 2:1 risk-reward
- Previous resistance levels
- Fibonacci extensions (127%, 162%)
- Trail stop below 20 EMA

## ðŸ’¡ Key Reminders
